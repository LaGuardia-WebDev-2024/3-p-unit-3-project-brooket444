Recipe #1 Creamy Tomato Pasta

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1: Grab a pot and boil water, make sure to add salt into your water

Step 2: Wash your grape tomatoes and peel your garlic

Step 3: After washing your grape tomatoes start cutting them in half

Step 4: Put aside the cut up grape tomatoes, now start peeling and cutting your garlic.

Step 5: Now put aside the cut up garlic and tomatoes

Step 6 Now add your pasta into the boiled water and let pasta cook for 8 minutes.

Step 7: Now we can start on the actual sauce. Start by grabbing another pot, pour cooking oil.
